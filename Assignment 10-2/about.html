More about Coldstone

Cold Stone Creamery is an American ice cream parlor chain. Headquartered in Scottsdale, Arizona, the company is owned and operated by Kahala Brands. The company's main product is premium ice cream made with approximately 12–14% butterfat, made on location and customized for patrons at time of order.

Our Diversity Strategy

Promoting high levels of employee engagement and individual commitment to create a work environment that brings out the best in our people. Maintaining a strong focus on a demographically-balanced employee base by collaborating internally and externally with our culture through recruiting efforts and developing our people;. Leveraging internal resources to increase business opportunities with key vendors to allow supplier diversity in emerging and existing markets; and Providing our employees and franchisees with effective diversity training and experiences through a variety of learning approaches tailored for today's workforce needs.
