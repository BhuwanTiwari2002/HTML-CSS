Meat Processing and Butcher Shop

Since 1959 we have offered in-house custom slaughtering and full custom processing of beef and pork. We also offer year round venison and wild game processing featuring over 30 award winning sausage products. Other processing services include curing and smoking of hams, sausage, bacon, fish, and poultry. Haen Meats, located in Kaukauna, is a full-service meat market and we do have a full retail store. We offer gift boxes that are readily available during the holiday season and available by order year round. We service many Fox Cities businesses and restaurants and do offer wholesale pricing, grill rentals, and delivery for corporate events or charity organizations.
