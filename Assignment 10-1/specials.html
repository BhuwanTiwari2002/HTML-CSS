Ingredients

  2. 2 lbs. HAEN PORK TENDERLOIN cut in 1/2" slices
  3. 2 T. olive oil
  4. 1 T. butter
  5. 1/4 lb. ham and/or bacon
  6. 1/2 tsp. sage
  7. 1 small onion, chopped
  8. 4 T. sundried tomatoes, chopped
  9. 1/2 cup chicken broth
  10. 1/2 cup half & half
  11. shittake mushrooms
  12. salt and pepper

Methods/steps

  1. Precook bacon if desired.
  2. Heat oil and butter in a medium saucepan.
  3. Add ham and/or bacon, sage, tomato, onion, garlic, and mushrooms if desired.
  4. Saute over medium heat about 5 minutes.
  5. Add pork; saute about 10 minutes or until pork is lightly browned.
  6. Add broth and half and half.
  7. Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer about 20 minutes; sauce will thicken.
  8. Salt and pepper to taste.

Ingredients

  2. 1 can (10 oz.) refrigerated pizza crust
  3. 1 can (7.5 oz.) chili with beans (or use HAEN’S CHILI!)
  4. 1/4 cup drained sweet pickle relish
  5. 1 cup chopped Italian plum tomatoes
  6. 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese

Methods/steps

  1. Heat oven to 425°.
  2. Grease 12 inch pizza pan.
  3. Unroll dough; place on pan and press dough out to edge of pan, forming 1/2" rim.
  4. Bake 7-9 minutes until light golden brown.
  5. Remove partially baked crust; spoon chili over crust.
  6. Top chili with hot dogs, relish, tomatoes and cheese.
  7. Bake 15-18 minutes or until crust is a deep golden brown.
